Game rules

Objective

Win condition: move all 15 of your pieces into your home board, then bear off every piece before your opponent.

Board Direction and Movement

Opposite directions: players travel around the board in opposite ways. White moves from lower-numbered points toward higher-numbered points, while Black moves from higher-numbered points toward lower-numbered points.

Using dice: on your turn, roll and spend available dice values to make legal moves. A move is only legal if the destination is open according to game rules.

Turn Sequence

  1. Roll dice at the start of your turn.
  2. Select a piece from a board point or the bar.
  3. Move to highlighted legal points using die values.
  4. End turn when no legal moves remain.

Capturing and the Bar

Capture rule: landing on a point with exactly one opposing piece captures it and sends it to the bar.

Bar priority: if you have any pieces on the bar, you must re-enter those pieces before moving other pieces. Re-entry must follow a legal die value and open destination.

Bearing Off

When allowed: you can bear off only after all of your remaining pieces are inside your home board.

How to bear off: use legal dice to move pieces off the board. The first player to bear off all 15 pieces wins the game.
